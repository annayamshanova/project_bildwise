Frequently asked
questions

What types of construction projects do you handle?

We work on residential homes, commercial buildings, office fit-outs, renovations, and exterior upgrades — from small improvements to full ground-up construction.

How long will my project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. We’ll provide a detailed schedule after our initial consultation and keep you updated at every stage.

Do you provide cost estimates before starting?

Yes. We prepare a detailed estimate that includes labor, materials, and approximate timelines so you understand the investment before any work begins.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Our team holds all required licenses and carries comprehensive insurance to protect both you and your property throughout the project.

Can you work with my own architect or designer?

Yes, we frequently collaborate with external architects and designers. We’ll coordinate closely with them to keep the project aligned with your vision and budget.

Do you offer eco-friendly building options?

We can incorporate energy-efficient materials, improved insulation, low-VOC finishes, and other sustainable solutions to reduce environmental impact and running costs.
